Business Data Sources Review

In a mordern Business environment there are several sources of data.

**1.Internal Operational Databases.**

Here is where most core business data lives. These are often RDBMS.

     *Entity Resource Planning(ERP):Centralised data on operations,finance and HR

     *Customer Relationship Management(CRM):Interactions with otential and current customers.(eg Salesforce)

     *Transactional Databases:Realtime records on inventory and sales updates.(eg MySQL and PostgreSQL)

 **2.External Third-Party Data**
 
 This is data that is sourced from outside the organisation to provide market context.

    *Social Media APIs:Monitoring trends.(eg X and LinkedIn)

    *Financial Market Data:Mainly stock prices and exchange rates.

    *Public Datasets:Government census data.

 **3.Semi-Structured and Unstructured sources.**

 Not all data fits nearly into rows and columns.

    *Log Files:System logs and web server logs.

    *IoT and Sensors: Data from manufacturing equipment, GPs trackers, or smart devices.

    *Document Stores: PDFs, emails and Excel spred sheets

 **4.SaaS Applications.**
 
  Cloud based tools. Data is stored in their own silos.

    *Marketing Platforms: Google Ads, Mailchimp

    *Support Desks:Zendesk
